<h1><a id="TensorFlow_W_tensorflowstream_executorplatformdefaultdso_loadercc59_0"></a>[TensorFlow] 运行报错：W tensorflow/stream_executor/platform/default/dso_loader.cc:59]</h1>
<h3><a id="_2"></a>问题描述</h3>
<p>使用官网的命令下载TensorFlow没有出现问题</p>
<pre><code class="prism language-bash">pip <span class="token function">install</span> tensorflow
</code></pre>
<p>但是运行时出现报错信息：</p>
<pre><code>2020-09-20 21:31:45.455714: W tensorflow/stream_executor/platform/default/dso_loader.cc:59] Could not load dynamic library 'libcudart.so.10.1'; dlerror: libcudart.so.10.1: cannot open shared object file: No such file or directory
2020-09-20 21:31:45.455738: I tensorflow/stream_executor/cuda/cudart_stub.cc:29] Ignore above cudart dlerror if you do not have a GPU set up on your machine.
</code></pre>
<h3><a id="_17"></a>问题分析</h3>
<p>看报错信息也能看出来时GPU的问题。</p>
<p>查阅资料后时GPU的版本与TensorFlow的版本不一致。</p>
<p>对应关系参见：https://tensorflow.google.cn/install/source#gpu</p>

<p>我用的是<strong>CUDA 10.0</strong>，对应TensorFlow版本应为 <strong>tensorflow-2.0.0</strong></p>
<h3><a id="_47"></a>解决方案</h3>
<p>下载对应的TensorFlow版本</p>
<pre><code class="prism language-bash">pip <span class="token function">install</span> tensorflow<span class="token operator">==</span>2.0.0 -i https://pypi.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/simple
</code></pre>
<p>下载后正常！</p>
